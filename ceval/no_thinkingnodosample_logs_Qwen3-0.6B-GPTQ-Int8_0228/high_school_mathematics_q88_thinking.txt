$$

$$
(2a + 2i)(1 - i) = 2a(1 - i) + 2i(1 - i) = 2a - 2ai + 2i - 2i^2

$$
2a - 2ai + 2i + 2 = 2a + 2 + (2i - 2ai)
$$

$$
2a + 2 = 0 \Rightarrow a = -1
