### 第一步：展开 $\left(x - \frac{2}{x}\right)^6$

使用二项式定理：

$$
\left(x - \frac{2}{x}\right)^6 = \sum_{k=0}^{6} \binom{6}{k} x^{6 - k} \left(-\frac{2}{x}\right)^k
$$

化简后：

$$
= \sum_{k=0}^{6} \binom{6}{k} (-2)^k x^{6 - k - k} = \sum_{k=0}^{6} \binom{6}{k} (-2)^k x^{6 - 2k}
$$

### 第二步：乘以 $\left(x^2 + x + 1\right)$

我们先考虑 $\left(x^2 + x + 1\right)$ 与 $\left(x - \frac{2}{x}\right)^6$ 的乘积。

设：

$$
(x^2 + x + 1) \cdot \left(x - \frac{2}{x}\right)^6 = \sum_{k=0}^{6} \binom{6}{k} (-2)^k x^{6 - 2k} \cdot x^2 + \sum_{k=0}^{6} \binom{6}{k} (-2)^k x^{6 - 2k} \cdot x + \sum_{k=0}^{6} \binom{6}{k} (-2)^k x^{6 - 2k} \cdot 1
$$

合并后得到：

$$
= \sum_{k=0}^{6} \binom{6}{k} (-2)^k x^{6 - 2k + 2} + \sum_{k=0}^{6} \binom{6}{k} (-2)^k x^{6 - 2k + 1} + \sum_{k=0}^{6} \binom{6}{k} (-2)^k x^{6 - 2k}

$$
x^{6 - 2k + 2} = x^0 \quad \Rightarrow \quad 6 - 2k + 2 = 0 \quad \Rightarrow \quad 8 - 2k = 0 \quad \Rightarrow \quad k = 4
