$$
\frac{2\sin\alpha\cos\alpha}{3\cos\alpha - 1} = \frac{\sin\alpha}{\cos\alpha}
$$

$$
2\sin\alpha\cos\alpha = \frac{\sin\alpha}{\cos\alpha}(3\cos\alpha - 1)
$$

$$
2\cos\alpha = 3 - \frac{1}{\cos\alpha}

$$
2\cos^2\alpha = 3\cos\alpha - 1

$$
2\cos^2\alpha - 3\cos\alpha + 1 = 0

$$
