$$ \vec{F} = I \cdot (\text{lens area}) \times B $$

- Length of the wire ($\ell$): 0.20 meters,
- Current in the wire ($I$: 2.0 A),
- Magnetic induction strength ($B_{max}$): $5.0\times10^{-3}\,\mathrm{T}$,

Substituting into the equation:

$$ 
\vec{F}_{total} = 2.0~\mathbf{A} \times 0.20~\mathit{\rm m} \times 5.0\times^{10}-3\,\mathrm{T} \\
$$ 

Calculating step-by-step yields:

$$ 
2.0 \times 0.679 + ... = 2.0 \times {10}^{-3}\,\mathrm{Nm/A} =
\boxed{2.0 \times }{10}^{-3}\,\mathrm{N}.
$$
